# 📊 SALES ANALYTICS DASHBOARD


## 📌 PROJECT SUMMARY

The **Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard** is a dynamic business intelligence project designed using **Power BI** to analyze sales performance across different branches, cities, products, and customer segments.

This dashboard helps convert complex sales records into easy-to-understand visual insights that support business growth and strategic decision-making.

The project focuses on identifying sales trends, customer preferences, revenue distribution, and product performance using interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 AIM OF THE PROJECT

This project was developed to provide a complete overview of retail business performance through data analytics and visualization.

### Main Goals:

* Analyze overall revenue performance
* Track branch and city sales trends
* Understand customer purchase behavior
* Compare product category performance
* Monitor preferred payment methods
* Create interactive business reports

## 📂 DATA DESCRIPTION

The dataset contains retail transaction details such as:

* Sales Transactions
* Product Categories
* Branch Details
* City Information
* Customer Gender
* Payment Types
* Quantity Purchased
* Product Ratings
* Revenue & Tax Details

The dataset was processed and transformed before visualization to ensure accurate reporting and analysis.

## 📈 ANALYTICAL INSIGHTS

* Branch **C** achieved the highest sales performance.
* Cash transactions contributed the largest share of payments.
* Food and beverage products generated maximum revenue.
* Sales distribution across cities remained balanced.
* Female customer purchases slightly exceeded male customer purchases.
